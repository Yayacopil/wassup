Our Story

Crafting Quality Frames Since 2024

Wallypop was born from a passion for preserving memories and showcasing art in the best possible way. What started as a small workshop has grown into a trusted name in premium picture framing.

Our founder saw the need for high-quality, affordable frames that could elevate any artwork or photograph without distracting from its beauty.

Our Craftsmanship

Each Wallypop is meticulously crafted using only the finest materials. We source sustainable woods, premium metals, and museum-quality glass to ensure your artwork is protected and displayed beautifully.

Our team of skilled artisans combines traditional techniques with modern technology to create frames that stand the test of time while complementing contemporary interiors.
